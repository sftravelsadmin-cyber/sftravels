## 🗺️ Section 2: Recommended Travel Itineraries

### 1. Mysore Royal Heritage Tour (1–2 Days)
*   *Route*: Bangalore -> Srirangapatna -> Mysore Palace -> Brindavan Gardens -> Chamundi Hills -> Return.
*   *Highlight*: Witness the historic royal grandeur, gorgeous illuminated palaces, and historic temples of the old capital.
*   *Recommended Vehicles*: Hatchback, Sedan, or Innova SUV.

### 2. Ooty Queen of Hill Stations Tour (3–4 Days)
*   *Route*: Bangalore -> Bandipur National Park (Safari) -> Ooty Lake -> Botanical Gardens -> Doddabetta Peak -> Coonoor (Tea Gardens) -> Return.
*   *Highlight*: Escape to the cool, misty Nilgiri hills, lush tea estates, and beautiful scenic lakes.
*   *Recommended Vehicles*: Toyota Innova Crysta or Force Urbania (for groups).

### 3. Coorg Scotland of India Tour (3–4 Days)
*   *Route*: Bangalore -> Bylakuppe Golden Temple (Tibetan Monastery) -> Abbey Falls -> Madikeri Fort -> Raja’s Seat -> Talakaveri -> Return.
*   *Highlight*: Indulge in coffee plantation walks, misty valley viewpoints, beautiful cascading waterfalls, and rich local Kodava culture.
*   *Recommended Vehicles*: Toyota Innova Crysta or Tempo Traveller.
